## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free? What's the catch?**
A: Completely free to use. No credit card, no login, no trial. SpendLens is built by Credex, which sells discounted AI infrastructure credits. If your audit shows significant savings, we'll mention Credex as one option. If not, we won't. We'd rather give you an honest audit and earn your trust than manufacture savings to push a sale.

**Q: How current is the pricing data?**
A: We verify pricing against official vendor pages before each deployment. Every number in our engine has a source URL in our public PRICING_DATA.md file. If you spot something wrong, email us and we'll fix it within 24 hours.

**Q: Does the audit store my data?**
A: The audit engine runs on your inputs and returns results instantly. We store the audit result (tool names, plan names, savings numbers) to generate your shareable URL. We don't store your company name or email unless you choose to provide them for the report copy. The public share URL strips any identifying details.

**Q: How do the recommendations work? Is AI deciding this?**
A: The savings calculations are hardcoded rules, not AI-generated. We compare your plan tier, seat count, and use case against each vendor's published pricing and recommend downgrades or switches based on transparent criteria you can audit yourself. The 100-word summary paragraph is AI-generated (Claude) — but it describes the rule-based findings, not invents them.

**Q: What is Credex and why did they build this?**
A: Credex sources discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and others — from companies that overforecast or pivoted. The discount is real: typically 20–40% below retail. SpendLens is a free tool that helps startups understand where they're overspending. For teams with significant savings opportunities, Credex is one solution. For teams already spending well, we're just a useful free tool — and we're fine with that.
